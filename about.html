Strensham Mill, situated on Shakespeare's River Avon offers moorings and boats for sale near the M5 and M50, Worcester. Our marina is a relaxing getaway location in the Worcestershire countryside.

We have 20 years experience as a moorings provider and we have a variety of boats for sale.

Our moorings are state of the art design, anti slip platforms with high rise poles and mooring rings, to which our boats are tethered.

All our moorings are currently occupied. However, you can contact us and enquire about adding your name to the waiting list.

Some boats that we sell will include mooring options.
